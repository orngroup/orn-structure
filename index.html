TripFactory India — Organisational Structure

TripFactory operates across seven core departments, each with a defined mission, team structure, and set of roles. The Sales department is the commercial engine — split into B2C, B2B, and Tours verticals. Given the volume of escorted and group tours, TripFactory maintains a dedicated Tours Operations team separate from general Operations, ensuring specialist focus on complex multi-component itineraries.

Customer booking journey — how departments interact
1
Marketing

Awareness & Lead

Digital campaign, social content or word-of-mouth drives the client to TripFactory. Lead captured via web form, WhatsApp or trade agent referral and passed to Sales.

2
Sales Agent

Qualify, Propose & Close

Agent qualifies the enquiry, builds an itinerary proposal with correct margins, negotiates and collects the deposit. Booking file opened in the system immediately.

3
Operations

Flights Ticketed

Agent submits flight brief to the Flights Team. Tickets are issued and verified. Agent confirms details and adds e-tickets to the client file. Schedule changes monitored.

4
Visa Team

Visa Processing

Agent initiates the visa request. The Visa Team coordinates with the external partner, tracks progress bi-weekly and verifies the visa on receipt. Agent is updated throughout.

5
Tours Ops

Tour Confirmation

For escorted tours, Tours Ops manages the rooming list, group manifests, DMC briefings and Tour Leader assignment. Agent is the client's contact; Tours Ops runs the engine.

6
Sales Agent

Docs, Briefing & Departure

Agent collects the balance, compiles the travel document pack, conducts the pre-departure call and confirms all components. Client departs with full confidence.

7
Customer Services

In-trip Support

Agent handles frontline in-trip issues. Critical escalations go to Customer Services. CS manages complaints, supplier failures and after-hours emergencies with the on-call team.

8
Finance

Reconciliation & Close

Finance validates all supplier invoices, processes payables and reconciles the booking P&L. Trade agent commission is paid. Booking is financially closed with margin confirmed.

Key department handoffs & dependencies

💼 Sales Agent triggers

  • Submits flight brief → Flights Team actions it
  • Initiates visa request → Visa Team tracks it
  • Sends rooming list → Tours Ops builds the manifest
  • Escalates in-trip issue → Customer Services owns it
  • Closes booking → Finance reconciles and pays

🔄 Operations & Tours Ops feed back to Sales

  • Flights confirmed → Agent receives e-tickets & adds to pack
  • Tour manifest finalised → Agent communicates to client
  • Visa granted → Agent verifies and adds to pre-dep checklist
  • Schedule change → Agent contacts client and re-books
  • Supplier issue on-trip → Agent keeps client informed

💰 Finance monitors throughout

  • Deposit received → Finance records and issues receipt
  • Supplier invoice arrives → Verified vs agreed rates
  • Balance due → Finance alerts agent & chases if overdue
  • Amendment made → Cost impact calculated and recorded
  • Booking closed → P&L confirmed, commission processed

📣 Marketing & Sales alignment

  • Marketing generates leads → passed to correct sales team
  • Sales share conversion data → Marketing optimises spend
  • Sales flag product gaps → Marketing builds new campaigns
  • CS reviews feed into content → destination advice & FAQs
  • Repeat client data → CRM retargeting and loyalty comms

🎧 Customer Services escalation path

  • Frontline: Sales Agent handles all direct client contact
  • Level 1 escalation: CS Executive takes the case
  • Level 2 escalation: CS Manager + supplier management
  • After hours: On-call co-ordinator manages live emergencies
  • Post-trip: CS collects feedback, logs for supplier review

🗺️ Tours Ops — unique to TripFactory

  • Works directly with Tours Sales on capacity & availability
  • Manages DMC and ground operator relationships in-destination
  • Briefs and deploys Tour Leaders for each departure
  • Feeds post-tour reports back to product & sales teams
  • Flags pricing changes to Finance for contract renewal
